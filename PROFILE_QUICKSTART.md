# Personal Profile - Quick Start Guide

## Accessing Your Profile

1. Log in to your account
2. Click **"My Profile"** button in the top header (next to Sign Out)
3. Your profile editor opens

## Filling Out Your Profile

### Profile Photo (Optional)
- Click "Choose Photo"
- Select PNG or JPG image (max 2MB)
- Photo appears immediately in preview

### Basic Information
- ✅ Full Name (required)
- Date of Birth
- Gender

### Contact Details
- Mobile Number
- Alternate Number
- Work Phone
- Personal Email

### Address
- Address Line 1 & 2
- City, State, Country
- Pincode

### Professional Details (Engineers)
- Designation (e.g., Site Engineer, Senior Engineer)
- Years of Experience
- Reporting Manager (select from dropdown)
- Skills/Expertise (describe your skills)

### Social Links
- LinkedIn Profile URL
- Portfolio URL

## Saving Your Profile

1. Fill in the fields you want to update
2. Click **"Save Profile"** at the bottom
3. Success message appears
4. Click "Cancel" or X to close

## Read-Only Fields

These fields cannot be changed by you:
- ✅ Email (system-assigned)
- ✅ Role (admin-controlled)

Contact your administrator to change these.

---

## For HR: Viewing Engineer Profiles

1. Go to **HR Dashboard**
2. Click **"Profiles"** tab
3. See list of all engineers
4. Use search box to find specific engineers
5. Click on any engineer to view their full profile

**Note:** This is read-only view. Engineers manage their own profiles.

---

## Tips

- Keep your contact information up to date
- Add your professional skills to help HR understand your capabilities
- Upload a professional photo for better recognition
- Add LinkedIn profile for professional networking
- Update your experience and designation as you progress

---

## Need Help?

- Ensure you're logged in
- Check for any validation error messages
- Contact your administrator for email/role changes
- Report technical issues to system support

**That's it!** Your profile is now complete and visible to authorized users.
